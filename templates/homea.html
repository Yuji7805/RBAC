AL3RT.me is an implementation of the AL3RT framework. It is your personal, context-aware AI-assistant. It will respond to your prompts in the context of your discussion Stream – learning from and improving its personalized responses to your Prompts.
Download Google Chrome Extension
Installation and Use of the Google Chrome Extension

1.Download and unzip Extension to a folder on your computer

2.Launch your Google Chrome browser

3.Click on Extensions in the top right corner of the browser

4.Click Manage Extensions

5.Click on Load Unpacked, and go to the folder where you unzipped the downloaded zip file

6.The Extension’s Options screen appears. Add your Streams and Prompts for selection later when you use the extension. You can modify the Prompts later.

7.When done, restart the Google Chrome Browser.

8.Highlight any text on a web page in Chrome and the AL3RT logo will appear. Click on it to select the chat Stream and Prompt to submit the highlighted text to the AI engine.

9.Note: You can further edit to personalize the Prompt and selected (highlighted) text before submitting it to the AI engine.
